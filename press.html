Endearly

A quiet, private iOS keepsake for remembering the little things about the people you love.

What it is

Endearly is a personal notebook for the people who matter — a favorite flower, a shoe size, a past gift, an offhand wish. Everything lives only on your device: no account, no cloud, no ads, no tracking. Its App Store privacy label reads “Data Not Collected”, provably, because it is built without a server to collect anything. It is bought once, not rented by subscription. Deliberately, it is not a social network, not a CRM, and not another contacts app — it exists to help you be thoughtful, not productive.

The three things that make it different

Private

Private by architecture

No cloud, no account, no ads. “Data Not Collected” — a fact, not a promise.

Yours

Buy once, own it

One small payment, once. You pay with money, never with your attention or data.

Calm

Calm, like a notebook

No feed, no notifications for their own sake, no gamification.
