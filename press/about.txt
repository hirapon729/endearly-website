Endearly press kit
==================

Name
Endearly

One-liner
A private, offline iOS keepsake for remembering the little things about the people you love.

Platform
iPhone, iOS 17+

Price
Free for the first three people; one-time US$4.99 unlock for unlimited. No subscription.

Privacy
Data Not Collected. Endearly works offline, stores data only on the device, and requires no account. Nothing leaves the device.
