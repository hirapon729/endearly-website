Privacy

The short version: we collect nothing. There is nothing to leak, sell, or lose.

Endearly has no account, no cloud, no server, and no analytics — in the app or on this website. Everything you write stays on your device.

What the app collects

Nothing. The people, notes, dates, gifts, and photos you add are stored only on your iPhone. They ride your normal device backup (iPhone / iCloud) if you use one, and you can export an open, portable copy of everything at any time — it is yours to keep or move. Its App Store privacy label reads “Data Not Collected”, which is a fact about how the app is built, not a promise we ask you to trust.

What this website collects

Nothing. This page loads no analytics, sets no cookies, and makes no third-party requests. There is no tracking pixel and no advertising network. If you email us for support, we see only what you choose to write; we reply and then we are done.

Payments

If you unlock the full app, Apple handles the purchase. We never see your card, and Apple does not share your identity with us — we only learn that an anonymous unlock happened. There is no subscription.
