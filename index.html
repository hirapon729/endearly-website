Honest answers

The questions worth asking.

Is my data backed up?

Your data lives only on your device, so it rides your normal iPhone/iCloud device backup. You can also export an open, portable copy any time — it’s yours to keep or move.

What does “Restore Purchase” do?

If you get a new phone or reinstall, one tap restores your one-time unlock through your Apple ID. No account of ours is involved.

How do I move to a new phone?

Restore from your device backup, or export a copy from the old phone and import it on the new one. Two taps either way.

How much does it cost?

Free to start (your first three people). Unlock unlimited people once, forever — no subscription. Pricing is finalized at launch.

Do you track me on this website?

No. This page loads no analytics, no cookies, and makes no third-party requests. The privacy promise starts here.
